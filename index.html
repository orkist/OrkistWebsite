    Our Beginning

    Orkist was founded with the love of technology and what it can do to to help humans create. My passion has been technology for as long as I can remember. I also possess an undeniable desire to be free to walk a path less traveled.

    I began to study many pioneers in different areas of various eras. Although, the stories change, they all have similarities. These amazing people have the ability to stick to what they love. Trends and money came and went.
